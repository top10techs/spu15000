Things to consider before buying smartphone

Camera- Most people thought that higher the mega pixels (mp) of cameras,higher will be the camera quality. But this is not truth.Mega pixels are only responsible for details in the image.It has nothing to do with image quality. That is why iphones are better in quality even if they have less mega pixels. To buy a phone with better camera we have to look other things like camera sensor size(the larger,the better),camera focal length,camera sensor type etc.After seeing all these then we have to look for mega pixels i.e the priority of mega pixels is very less for quality of image.

RAM- Most people think that higher the size of RAM higher will be performance.But it is not truth.The performance of phone depends on RAM speed and memory type not on RAM capacity. RAM capacity helps us only in multi tasking.RAM capacity does not increase the performance of phone Higher the speed,higher will be the performance
